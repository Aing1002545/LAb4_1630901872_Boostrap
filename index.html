From the long-standing competition of BELIF+'s 'I-LAND' in collaboration with BigHit Entertainment and CJ ENM, the group now has a total of 7 final members with the group 'ENHYPEN'.

The group's name was introduced through the livestream of the final episode of I-Land. According to the etymology, Enhypan got its name from the (-) symbol that created new meanings, including "connection, discovery, meet and grow.” Similar to connecting words to find new meanings, Enhypan has a goal. "To connect, discover and grow together to create something new."

They made their debut on November 30, 2020.
